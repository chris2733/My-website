Hotelspeaker

    
This client wanted to create a new website for
    his startup based in Barcelona, called Hotelspeaker, offering a service to manage
    online reviews of your hotel. The current site
    needed a new design and structure, along with more pages and functionality,
    and Wordpress was chosen as the most suitable option.


    website

  


  

    
Details of work

    
I worked very closely with the client's established team to plan the work out.
      I worked with the lead developer to migrate the site from my test domain to their business domain with
    little to no down time, the graphics designer to implement their design across several
    iterations and the PR specialist to organise the blog area; once these major parts
    were in place, I ensured all others had their personal profiles onsite.


    
A challenging part of this site was ensuring 6 languages were implemented fully
      across the site including French, German, Dutch, Spanish and Catalan. This was
    perhaps the most time consuming part, and needed regular communication with specific
    team members for translations and corrections.

  




 


 








  


      

        
          
        
        
          
        
        
          
        
        
          

        
      

      

          
      


  


  


    
Wavelength Unisex Salon


      
This local client needed a website for their salon to attract more
        customers, and also needed help improving their online presence in general.
        I demonstrated how to buy the domain they needed and organise the right
        hosting plan, then worked with them to establish exactly what they had in mind.


        website

      


      

        
Details of work

        
The client already had a certain vision in mind, which was styled
          on other business owners websites she knew of. This consisted of a
          minimalistic look with stark black, white and blues, the same palette
          used in and on the shop front, also using a custom font, so I found the
          closest available style. They had also chosen two headshots to place on
          the home screen, which were then photoshopped to fit the theme.


        
Along with a new website, I helped establish their Google My Business and made
          sure all contact details and as many suitable photos they had were available,
          resulting in immediate results on their analytics. We then discussed possible
          results of using various social medias with their current reviews, after which
          we established a plan to develop and improve these as much as possible.

      




 


 








  


      

        
          
        
        
          
        
        
          
        
        
          

        
      

      

          
      


  


  


    
Littlefaces


      
Littlefaces is a UK charity which is setting up a school in rural Kenya,
        focusing on teaching children in a number of small villages in one area. a
        website was needed to show potential supporters exactly what they do, and
        updates on everything they are involved in, in particular what supplies
        they need to continue helping the children.


        website

      


      

        
Details of work

        
The client already had a certain vision in mind, which was styled
          on other business owners websites she knew of. This consisted of a
          minimalistic look with stark black, white and blues, the same palette
          used in and on the shop front, also using a custom font, so I found the
          closest available style. They had also chosen two headshots to place on
          the home screen, which were then photoshopped to fit the theme.


          
The organisation had hosting and a very basic static site setup, so I
            installed Wordpress in place of this, and am still in the progress of
            updating the site with content from the head of the charity and the
            editors. Along with content I am working closely with a graphics designer
            to create catching visuals to fit in with the theme and styles wanted by
            the client, based on the logo and colour palette created by us.

      




 


 








  


      

        
          
        
        
          
        
        
          
        
        
          

        
      

      

          
      


  


  


    
NS Designs Online


      
This client was an architect based in Pasadena, and wanted to create a
        website for his own firm. The client already had a basic domain setup,
        so Wordpress was installed and a theme was chosen to build upon.


        website

      


      

        
Details of work

        
Once the client was happy with the theme, I implemented the content that was
          provided into specific sections, giving potential customers the clear
          and simple message he wanted to convey, and any logos representing the
          firm were edited or recreated to ensure they fit well. Alongside this a SEO strategy
          was created to ensure the right customers were attracted, and I worked
          closely with another freelancer who specialised in PPC to bring more
          traffic to the site. 


        
The Google My Business page was also set up for the firm, linking their social
          media and reviews from Facebook and Houzz among others.

      




 


 










  


      

        
          
        
        
          
        
        
          
        
        
          

        
      

      

          
      


  


  


    
Cardiff MADE


      
Cardiff MADE is an community interest company based in Roath, Cardiff,
        who exhibit many different kinds of arts, crafts, music and foods made
        independently in the local area. They had been running for many years
        but without a webpage, so in my first meeting we reviewed their online
        presence as a whole, editing the social media profiles and Google Business
        as necessary and showing them how to purchase a simple domain and hosting
        package.


        website

      


      

        
Details of work

        
This is still a work in progress as is covered by a landing page, but I am
          working closely with the owner and designer to build the site on
          Wordpress with a plethora of features. Some of these include a directory of
          all registered artists with their biographies, an online shop to buy the art,
          a page for the in house cafe with menu and link to UberEats, a blog of news
          and upcoming exhibitions, current exhibitions on, and much more as it is developed.
